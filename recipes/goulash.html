Goulash

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Red Bell Peppers
  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Paprika
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Heat the oil up
  2. Put onions and peppers in oil
  3. Cook for 15 min
  4. Put meat in
  5. Cook 15 min
  6. Put water and potatoes
  7. Simmer 1h
